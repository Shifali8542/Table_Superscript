The book value of the Fund portfolio at 31 December 2017, Euro 806.7 million, shows an increase of Euro 331.3 million with respect to 31 December 2016 as a result of the acquisitions made in the year in the telecommunications and renewable energy sectors (Euro 236.2 million), the disbursement of Euro 14 million by way of a deferred payment for a minority stake in SIA, and valuation gains of Euro 81.1 million.
